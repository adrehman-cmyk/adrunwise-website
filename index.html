FAQ

Questions you probably have

Honest answers. No sales fluff.

QDo I need any technical knowledge?

None at all. I handle the entire technical setup. You just need to tell me how your business works and what problems you want to solve.

QHow long does the whole process take?

Most systems go live within 5–14 days depending on the package. The discovery call and proposal happen within 48 hours of your first message.

QWhat tools do I need to already have?

Usually none to start. I recommend the right tools based on your needs. However, third-party tool subscription costs (e.g. booking software, CRM, AI call platforms) are not included in the package fee and are the client's responsibility. These are discussed and agreed upfront so there are no surprises.

QWhat if I want changes after launch?

Minor tweaks in the first two weeks are included. Larger changes or additions are scoped as small add-ons at a fixed price.

QIs there a long-term contract?

No. Every engagement is a fixed-scope project with a clear deliverable and timeline. Pay the project fee, receive the system. Simple.

QWhat if it doesn't work for my business?

I only take on projects I'm confident will deliver results. If after the discovery call I don't think automation will meaningfully help you, I'll tell you honestly — and won't take your money.
